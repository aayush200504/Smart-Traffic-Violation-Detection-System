How It Works

🔍
Violation Detection
Computer vision algorithms analyze traffic camera feeds in real-time to detect helmetless riding, red light jumping, wrong lane driving and more.
OpenCV · Python
🔢
ANPR Integration
Automatic Number Plate Recognition reads Indian number plates (format: MH 12 MN 6565) under varied lighting and weather conditions.
OCR · Tesseract
📄
Digital e-Challan
System automatically calculates fine amount and generates a professional PDF invoice compliant with Indian Motor Vehicles Act.
ReportLab · SQLite
📧
Instant Notification
Fine invoice delivered immediately to the registered vehicle owner's email with PDF attachment and payment instructions.
SMTP · SMS API
💳
Online Payment
Integrated Razorpay payment gateway allows vehicle owners to pay fines instantly. Supports UPI, cards, net banking and wallets.
Razorpay · UPI
📊
Analytics Dashboard
Real-time analytics showing violation trends, fine collection data, enforcement efficiency metrics and geographical hotspots.
Flask · Chart.js
